Biography

Longquan Dai is an Assistant Professor in Intelligent Media Analysis Group (IMAG) at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST). He received his B.A., M.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Henan University of Technology (HAUT) in 2006, Shantou Univeristy (STU) in 2010 and National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2016, respetively. Before jointing IMAG, he was a senior engineer of Huawei Technologies Co. Ltd. His Master and Doctoral supervisors are Prof. Minfen Shen who was the dean of the College of Engineering and Prof. Xiaopeng Zhang a member of Sina-European Laboratory of Informatics and Applied Mathematics (LIAMA).

Research: Image Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Computational Photography
